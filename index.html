About the Project

The Multiple-Application Surveillance Vessel (MASV) is designed to provide real-time surveillance and monitoring capabilities for various applications, including security, environmental monitoring, and search and rescue operations.Our vessel is equipped with advanced sensors, cameras, and a radar system to ensure comprehensive coverage and data collection.With a user-friendly interface, operators can easily control the vessel and access live data feeds from anywhere.
